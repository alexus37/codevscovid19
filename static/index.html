# Name Address Risk value

Once you have uploaded your location history we compare your personal location history with our risk map. We continuosly update this map by computationlly combining movement profiles of infected contributors. Based on you personal path, we can compute a risk value that represents your chance of having gotten infected. If you crossed paths with an infected person or in general resided at places, which have been frequently traversed by infected people, your risk value increases.

Important

The risk value does not represent a scientifically verified quantity. At this point it is just a value that we compute in good faith. Additionally, please keep in mind that this is still demo data, based on published GPS tracks and does not directly correlate with the Corona disease.
